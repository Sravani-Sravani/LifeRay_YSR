Aarogyasri Healthcare Platform

Aarogyasri Healthcare platform is the underlying adaptable framework which empowers the Aarogyasri scheme functions. Ranging from Patient engagement to Hospital engagement and day-to-day operations and claims processing including managing and maintain Electronic Health records. With seamless integrations to various agencies to ensure the citizens receive their due healthcare. Also with the mobile technologies embedded into the platform making it digitally connected to the field level users on the go.
